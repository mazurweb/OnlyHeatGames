⚙️ Support & Elite Skills

⚙️ Invention — 1 to 120 | Unlock at 80 Div + Sm + Cr

Invention lets you augment weapons and armour with perks that provide massive passive bonuses. It's the most impactful skill for endgame DPS and efficiency.

StepHowNotes
1. UnlockReach 80 Divination, 80 Smithing, 80 CraftingThen speak to Doc at the Invention guild
2. Discover blueprintsResearch blueprints in the Invention guildUnlocks gizmos, augmentors, and components
3. Augment gearUse augmentor on weapons/armourGear now gains XP when used, levels up for XP
4. Make gizmosCombine components in a gizmo shellRNG-based perk combinations
5. Insert perksAdd gizmo to augmented gearPassive bonuses now active
Must-Have Perks
Weapon Perks:
  • Precise 6 — increases minimum hit by 9%
  • Aftershock 4 — AoE damage every 50K damage dealt
  • Genocidal — up to 7% extra damage on Slayer tasks
  • Eruptive 4 — increases ability damage
Armour Perks:
  • Biting 4 — increases crit chance
  • Crackling 4 — passive AoE damage
  • Impatient 4 — basics generate more adrenaline
  • Enhanced Devoted 4 — damage reduction proc

🔭 Dungeoneering — 1 to 120 | Not a Traditional Skill

Dungeoneering involves completing procedurally generated dungeon floors in Daemonheim (southeast of Al Kharid). It earns Dungeoneering tokens spent on upgrades including Chaotic weapons.

  • Can be done solo or in groups of up to 5 (groups give significantly more XP)
  • Complete floors in order. Reset when you've done all available floors for your level.
  • Rush mode: complete floors as fast as possible — more XP per hour but less immersive
  • Key rewards: Chaotic rapier/longsword/maul (T80 weapons, free for tokens), Chaotic crossbow, Scroll of life, Herbicide
  • Sinkholes: Weekly D&D near Daemonheim giving bonus Prayer and Dungeoneering XP

⚡ XP Boosts — Stack These Together

💡 Stack Strategy: The biggest XP gains come from combining multiple boost sources simultaneously. Save your most XP-intensive training for Double XP Live events and use as many boosts as possible at once.
🌟 Seasonal / Event Boosts
  • Double XP Live: 2× XP on most skills for a limited window. Biggest multiplier available. Save buyable supplies for this.
  • Bonus XP (BXP): Earned from various sources — applies on top of base XP until depleted.
  • Leagues II (mid-2026): Temporary mode with massive XP and loot boosts — great for bulk grinding.
  • Pulse Cores: Temporary XP boost shared to nearby players. Stack with DXP.
  • Cinder Cores: Similar to Pulse Cores — shorter duration, higher boost.
📅 Daily / Weekly Boosts
  • Daily Challenges: Free skill-specific XP every day. 3–5 tasks, claim from Challenge Mistress in Burthorpe or the interface.
  • Guthixian Cache (daily): Best XP/hr in Divination. Daily cap resets midnight UTC.
  • Big Chinchompa (daily): Firemaking D&D. Check the D&D interface.
  • Penguin Hide & Seek (weekly): Penguin points convert to any skill XP.
  • Tears of Guthix (weekly): XP in your lowest skill. Never skip this.
⚗️ Permanent Boosts (Auras & Items)
  • Wisdom Aura: Flat 2.5% XP boost. Use during active training sessions.
  • Penance Aura: Restores Prayer during combat — enables using better prayers without banking.
  • Maniacal Aura: Increases critical hit chance — more kills per hour = more XP.
  • Brawling Gloves: 3× XP in Wilderness for specific skills.
  • Incense Sticks: Skill-specific % XP boosts. Bloodweed for Ranged, Spirit Weed for Summoning, etc.
🎓 Quest XP Rewards
  • The World Wakes: 450,000 XP in free lamps (any skill) + 110K Slayer + 250K Prayer
  • Violet is Blue + Too: Herblore lamps → Level 31 for free
  • Waterfall Quest: Attack + Strength both → Level 30 for free
  • The Knight's Sword: Smithing → Level 29 for free
  • Swept Away / Myths of the White Lands: Farming XP lamps

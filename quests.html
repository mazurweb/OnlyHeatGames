🟢 Starter Quests — No Requirements

📍 Start Here: All of these can be done immediately after arriving in Lumbridge. Zero skill or quest requirements. Do every single one before worrying about combat leveling.

🟡 Early Game — Combat Level 10–30

💡 Goal: Unlock Summoning, Herblore, and Thieving. These three skills are gated behind intro quests. Do these before grinding your combat stats higher.

🟠 Mid Game — Combat Level 30–60

🎯 Goal: Dragon Slayer unlocks Rune armour — the biggest gear milestone before membership kicks in. Waterfall Quest gives enormous Melee XP for free. Do these before you outgrow the content.

🔴 Late Game — Combat Level 60–80

⚠️ Members Required: Almost all late-game quests require membership. If you haven't subscribed yet, this is when it becomes essential. Desert Treasure and the Plague City chain are the two biggest unlocks here.

💀 Endgame Quests — Combat Level 80+

🏆 The World Wakes is the most important quest in this tier — 450,000 XP in free lamps, 110k Slayer XP, 250k Prayer XP, and it unlocks the Sunshine (Magic) and Death's Swiftness (Ranged) ultimates. Work toward this as your primary endgame quest goal.

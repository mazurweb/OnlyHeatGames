Essential Tips for New Players
⚔️ Start with Necromancy
The newest combat style is the most beginner-friendly. Self-contained gear progression, guided by the Kili Row quest chain. Free to play up to level 20.
📜 Quest Early, Grind Later
Quests give enormous XP rewards and unlock skills you can't train otherwise (Herblore, Summoning, Thieving, Runecrafting). Do quests before grinding anything.
⚡ Always Have a Slayer Task
Slayer trains your combat, earns gold, and fills your collection log simultaneously. Get a task from Jacquelyn in Lumbridge after your first few quests.
🗺️ Activate Every Lodestone
Lodestones are free permanent teleports. Every time you reach a new city, activate its lodestone. They're your travel network for the entire game.
📅 Daily Challenges = Free XP
Log in every day and complete your Daily Challenges. They reset at midnight UTC and give skill-specific XP for minimal effort. Never skip them.
🛡️ Always Use Power Armour
In RS3, Power Armour has an offensive bonus that increases your damage. Tank Armour does not. Always choose Power Armour — it makes you kill faster.

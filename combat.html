Combat & Fighting Leveling

RS3 has four combat styles. Pick one as your primary focus and master it before branching out. The February 2026 rework raised all caps to 120 and cleaned up bloated ability bars.

💀 Necromancy — Recommended for Beginners
Self-contained gear progression guided by quests. No ammo, no runes. F2P to level 20. Best XP/kill ratio at all stages. Huge survivability with Soul Split. Start here.
⚔️ Melee — Close-Range King
Attack (accuracy), Strength (damage), Defence (armour tier). Post-rework has cleaner ability rotations and better AoE Slayer options. Great for Slayer and mid-game bossing.
🏹 Ranged — On-Hit Effect Specialist
2026 rework focused Ranged on on-hit effects. Imbue Gales and Imbue Shadows add new combo mechanics. Excellent AoE with chinchompas. High DPS ceiling at endgame.
🔮 Magic — Crit & Burn Builds
2026 rework gave Magic clearer identity: crit builds and burn builds. Concentrated Blast now boosts crit chance earlier. Sunshine is one of the most powerful ultimates in the game.
⚠️ Critical Rule: Always use Power Armour, never Tank Armour. Power armour has an offensive bonus that directly increases your damage. Tank armour does not. Always use two-handed or dual-wield weapons — shields are situational only. Always exploit monster weaknesses using the combat triangle.

🔺 The Combat Triangle

RS3 uses an "affinity" system. Hitting a monster with its weakness dramatically increases your accuracy and damage. Always check the monster's weakness in the bestiary or by hovering over it.

⚔️ Melee → Ranged
Melee beats Ranged monsters. Stab, slash, or crush based on the specific weakness. Most Ranged-weak monsters are weak to one specific melee style.
🏹 Ranged → Magic
Ranged beats Magic monsters. Bolts or arrows — check if specific ammo type is preferred. Many Magic-weak monsters are in dungeons and slayer locations.
🔮 Magic → Melee
Magic beats Melee monsters. Use spells from your active spellbook. Ancient Magicks (Desert Treasure) has powerful ice spells for binding melee monsters.
💡 Affinity Values: A weakness means 90% affinity (high hit chance). Same triangle style = 65%. Off-triangle = 45–55%. The difference is massive — always fight monsters that your style is strong against.

💀 Necromancy — Full Level Guide

🌟 Best for New Players: Necromancy is designed to be the most accessible combat style. The Kili Row quest chain walks you through every gear upgrade. No ammo, no runes, large built-in healing via Soul Split. Start here.
1–20Tutorial — Lumbridge Catacombs & Draynor~5K xp/hr▼

Do Necromancy! quest first to unlock the skill and receive your Death Guard T10. Train on skeletons and zombies in Lumbridge Catacombs (unlocked via The Blood Pact). Use basic abilities with Revolution. Pick up Soul Searching quest to continue the lore chain. F2P players cap at level 20 — get membership to continue past here.

  • Use your Death Guard T10 from the quest
  • Keep learning conjures — skeletons first, then zombies
  • Activate Revolution mode — let the game auto-fire basics
  • Focus on completing the early Kili quests over pure grinding
20–40Kili Row Quest Chain — Upgrade your Death Guard~15–30K xp/hr▼

This level range is almost entirely quest-driven. Complete Kili Row at 20 (Death Guard T20), Kili's Craft at 30 (Death Guard T30). Between quests, train at Lumbridge Catacombs on higher-tier undead or move to Mort Myre Swamp undead. Collect necroplasm from your rituals at Fort Forinthry.

  • Always prioritize the next Kili quest over grinding
  • Set up your Ritual Site at Fort Forinthry for passive necroplasm
  • Use your conjures (skeleton warrior + bloated zombie) actively
  • Pick up Bones to Peaches / Cremation so you never need food
40–60Armoured Phantoms — Fort Forinthry~40–60K xp/hr▼

Armoured Phantoms inside Fort Forinthry are the best training spot at this tier. They're aggressive, stack well, and have good drops. Complement with Slayer tasks using Turael or Mazchna. Upgrade to Deathwarden Robe T40 from the City of Um shop.

  • Armoured Phantoms (Fort Forinthry basement) — best XP/hr here
  • Slayer: Mazchna tasks for combat + Slayer XP simultaneously
  • Continue ritual collection — you'll need lots of necroplasm
  • Unlock the Soulbound Lantern at 50 for DPS boost
60–80Well of Souls + Higher Slayer~60–100K xp/hr▼

Upgrade to Deathwarden Robe T70 from City of Um. Train at the Well of Souls for passive necromancy XP via rituals, and fight undead in Morytania. Slayer with Chaeldar or Duradel gives Slayer XP alongside Necromancy. Unlock Soul Split at 92 Prayer for near-infinite sustain.

  • Well of Souls rituals for passive bonus XP
  • Vyrewatch in Darkmeyer (semi-AFK, good XP)
  • Slayer with Chaeldar — tasks align well with Necromancy style
  • Priority: Complete City of Um questline to unlock T70+ gear
80–99Abyssal Lords (AFK King)~100–150K xp/hr▼

Abyssal Lords in the Senntisten asylum are the best semi-AFK combat training in the game. They auto-aggro, die quickly to Necromancy, and drop decent loot. Pair with Soul Split for unlimited sustain. Vyrewatch Sentinels in Darkmeyer are a solid alternative with slightly lower XP but easier access.

  • Abyssal Lords (Senntisten Asylum) — auto-aggro, great XP and drops
  • Vyrewatch Sentinels (Darkmeyer) — AFK with Blisterwood or Necro
  • Keep doing Slayer with Laniakea (L85) or Morvran for points
  • Upgrade to Deathwarden T80 or full Vestments of Death
99–120City of Um Endgame + Rasial~150–250K xp/hr▼

Push to 120 Necromancy via Abyssal Lords, Rasial bossing, and high-level Slayer. Rasial, the First Necromancer is the capstone Necromancy boss — great loot including the Soulbound Lantern (T95) and Deathguard (T95). Target Omid for T90 Conjures upgrade.

  • Rasial boss — best Necromancy-specific drops
  • Slayer with Laniakea — Abyssal Lords, Shades, top Slayer monsters
  • City of Um enhancements — upgrade rituals for passive XP
  • Complete Vessel of the Harbinger for BiS gear chain

Core Ability Rotation

Necromancy abilities stack Necrosis and Residual Souls. Your goal is to generate stacks and spend them on empowered abilities.

  • Touch of Death — generates Necrosis stacks. Use on cooldown.
  • Death Skulls — bounces between enemies. Strong AoE.
  • Finger of Death — high single-target damage. Spends Residual Soul stacks.
  • Soul Strike — stuns, generates stacks. Use to open fights.
  • Volley of Souls — AoE burst using Soul stacks. Priority on grouped enemies.
  • Living Death (Ultimate) — enhances abilities and generates huge stacks.
  • Conjure Skeleton Warrior — permanent passive DPS. Always keep active.
  • Conjure Zombie — passive tank. Useful for dangerous content.

⚔️ Melee — Full Level Guide

2026 Rework: Melee now has cleaner sub-style identities. Threshold system removed. Focus on building adrenaline with basics and spending on strong threshold-equivalent abilities. Slayer is the best way to train Melee.
1–30Lumbridge + Stronghold of Security~10–25K xp/hr▼

Start in the Lumbridge Catacombs after completing The Blood Pact. Move to Giant Spiders in the Stronghold of Security (Barbarian Village, south of Varrock) around level 15 — they're aggressive and give good XP. Free food is available from the Stronghold reward. Do Waterfall Quest as soon as possible for a massive Attack+Strength dump to level 30 in both skills.

  • Lumbridge Catacombs (L1–15) — skeletons and zombies
  • Giant Spiders, Barbarian Village Stronghold (L15–30) — aggressive, great XP
  • Do Waterfall Quest immediately — jumps Attack AND Strength to 30
  • Set to Shared XP or Strength priority mode
30–50Slayer + Chaos Druids~30–50K xp/hr▼

This is where Slayer takes over as your primary training method. Assign tasks from Jacquelyn in Lumbridge (easier tasks) or Mazchna in Canifis (harder tasks). Chaos Druids west of Edgeville are great for herbs + decent combat XP. Pick up your first Dragon weapon when you reach level 60 Attack.

  • Slayer tasks from Jacquelyn/Mazchna — always have a task active
  • Chaos Druids (Edgeville dungeon) — herbs pay for supplies
  • Upgrade armour to Mithril → Adamant → Rune as levels allow
  • Do Dragon Slayer quest to unlock Dragon and Rune armour
50–70Slayer Priority + Fire Giants~50–80K xp/hr▼

Slayer is now your primary training method. Use Duradel or Sumona for tasks. Fire Giants in the Waterfall dungeon or Brimhaven Dungeon are excellent for the non-Slayer periods. Upgrade to Dragon gear at 60 Attack and Bandos armour at 70 Defence.

  • Slayer with Duradel (requires 100 combat, 50 Slayer) — great tasks
  • Fire Giants (Waterfall dungeon) — bones for Prayer, decent XP
  • Upgrade to Dragon Scimitar (60 Attack) or Dragon 2h
  • Upgrade armour to Dragon at 60 Def, Bandos at 70 Def
70–90Slayer + GWD1 + Abyssal Demons~80–150K xp/hr▼

At 85 Slayer you unlock Abyssal Demons — one of the best Slayer tasks. Drygore weapons at 85 Attack provide the highest DPS available. At 80 Defence, upgrade to Torva armour or Anima Core of Zaros. Start GWD1 farming for Bandos and Armadyl gear if you want upgrades.

  • Slayer with Laniakea (L85 Slayer, 80 combat) — top-tier tasks
  • Abyssal Demons (85 Slayer) — Whip + charms + great drops
  • Drygore weapons — cheapest T90 DPS in game, highly recommended
  • Torva or Anima Core of Zaros armour at 80 Defence
90–120GWD2, ED, Nex + High Slayer~150–300K xp/hr▼

Endgame Melee is built around GWD2 bosses (Vindicta, Helwyr, Twin Furies, Gregorovic), Nex, and Elite Dungeons trash mob farming. Upgrade to Vestments of Havoc (T95, available from bosses) and Ek-Zekkil or Leng Swords for maximum DPS.

  • GWD2 bosses for T90 drops and Dormant Anima Core gear
  • Nex: Angel of Death (team) for top-tier armour pieces
  • Elite Dungeons 1–3 for tokens and XP on trash mobs
  • Rune Dragons (95 Slayer) — best gold per Slayer task

🔮 Magic — Full Level Guide

2026 Rework Identity: Magic is now built around crit builds and burn builds. Concentrated Blast now boosts crit chance (useful earlier). Sonic Wave reduces the adrenaline cost of your next attack by 60% — making Sunshine setups easier. Combust + Dragon Breath = core burn combo.
1–40Lumbridge Catacombs & Chaos Druids~10–25K xp/hr▼
  • Complete Rune Mysteries + Rune Memories for early Magic XP boost
  • Lumbridge Catacombs undead with Air Strike → Air Bolt → Air Blast
  • Chaos Druids (Edgeville dungeon, 45 Thieving for shortcut) — fast kills
  • Use the best Air spell you can — upgrade staffs as Magic level allows
  • Staff of Air or Air Battlestaff for unlimited air rune supply
40–70Polypore Dungeon Fungal Mages~30–60K xp/hr▼
  • Polypore Dungeon (Morytania area) — Fungal Mages are magic-weak → use Ranged actually. Use Melee or Necromancy instead here if possible.
  • Alternative: Rock Lobsters in Waterbirth Island Dungeon
  • Ice Burst / Blood Burst when unlocked via Desert Treasure
  • Upgrade to Mystic robes (40 Magic), then Subjugation robes (70 Magic)
  • Desert Treasure quest at 50+ Magic unlocks Ancient Magicks — huge upgrade

  • Slayer with Magic: targets weak to magic (many mid-Slayer creatures)
  • Anima Core of Zamorak armour (T80) — best affordable Magic power armour
  • Staff of Sliske (T80) — solid non-degradeable weapon
  • Learn the Sunshine → Sonic Wave → Concentrated Blast rotation
  • GWD1 Zamorak — magic weakness, good loot and XP

⚡ The Ability System

RS3 uses an ability-based combat system called the Evolution of Combat (EoC). Abilities use and generate Adrenaline — your primary combat resource.

Basic Abilities
Generate adrenaline. Low damage but fast cooldowns. Make up the majority of your rotation. Used to build toward more powerful abilities.
Powerful Abilities
Post-rework replacement for Thresholds. Require adrenaline to use. High damage. These are your key damage dealers.
Ultimate Abilities
Require 100% adrenaline. Massive damage or powerful effects. Key ultimates: Berserk (Melee), Death's Swiftness (Ranged), Sunshine (Magic), Living Death (Necromancy).
💡 Revolution++ Mode: Set Combat Mode to Revolution++ in Settings → Gameplay → Combat. This auto-fires abilities from your action bar for you, letting you learn the system gradually. Set bar size to 11+ to include all ability types.
